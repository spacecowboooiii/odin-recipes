Bacon Wrapped Shrimp

One of the most easiest, most delicious appetizers you can make.

Small appetizer that doesn't require much preparation and seasoning and can be served with a dipping sauce such as remoulde sauce.

You need the following ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Large Peeled Shrimp
  • 1 Tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground pepper

Steps to Make Bacon Wrapped Shrimp:

  1. Preheat oven to 425ËšF.
  2. Place bacon on rack and bake for 5 to 10 minutes depending on thickness.
  3. Place shrimp in a bowl and add olive oil, salt and pepper. Toss until all is coated.
  4. When bacon is cool enough, wrap bacon around one raw shrimp. Secure with a toothpick, repeat with each shrimp then place in oven again.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes until shrimp has a pink and bacon is cooked well, then done!
