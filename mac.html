Mac and Cheese

A dish of cooked macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce, most commonly cheddar.

A dish of pasta and cheese sauce, traditionally using macaroni and cheddar, baked in a casserole or sometimes cooked on a stovetop

You need the following ingredients:

  • Macaroni Pasta
  • Salted Butter
  • Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • Milk

Steps to make Mac and Cheese:

  1. In a small pot, boil about 2 cups of water, then when it's boiling add the pasta
  2. In a seperate pot, stir together some butter and flour, afterwards whisk in some milk until the sauce is thick and then add some cheese
  3. Add some salt, pepper, and a little bit of cayenne and nutmeg for your liking to give it some taste
  4. Drain out the pasta once its cooked well enough. After the pasta is dry, pour the cheese sauce over and stir and done!
