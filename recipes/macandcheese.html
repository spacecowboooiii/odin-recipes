Indgredients

  • Elbow Pasta
  • Preferred cheese of choice (Velvetta)
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil elbow pasta over medium flame
  2. Empty water over strainer and let dry for a minute
  3. Add butter and mix until dry
  4. Add milk and mix
  5. Add velvetta cheese and mix until creamy
