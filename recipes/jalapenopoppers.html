Jalapeno Poppers

Description

Jalapeno Poppers are a good side snack for when watching a game or movie.

Indgredients

  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Jalapeno
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cut jalapeno in half, remove insides
  2. Stuff inside with cheese, then wrap with bacon
  3. Place in oven at 350 degrees
