Indgredients

  • Shrimp
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Jalapeno
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cut shrimp into butterfly
  2. Cut jalapeno and cheese into thin strings
  3. Place jalapeno and cheese into shrimp then wrap with bacon
  4. Place melted butter into oven pan, place shrimp on top
  5. Bake at 42 degrees for 15 minutes
