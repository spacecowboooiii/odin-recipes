Jalapenos Poppers

A jalapeno pepper that has been stuffed with cheese and wrapped in bacon.

Jalapeno sliced in half and stuff with either cream cheese, sometimes a cheese blend - and wrapped in bacon and heated.

You need the following ingredients:

  • Jalapeno Pepper
  • Cream Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps to make Jalapeno Poppers

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degree F.
  2. Mix the cream cheese and cheddar together until evenly blended and the fill the sliced jalapeno with the mixture. Repeat for all jalapenos.
  3. Wrap jalapeno with the slice of bacon and place all on a sheet of aluminum.
  4. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, until bacon is crispy, and done!
